Body Fat Percentage
body fat percentage is the percentage of fat that the body contains.
Body Fat Formula
X :(Body weight x 1.082) + 94.42
Y :Waist measurement x 4.15
Lean Body Mass: X - Y
Body Fat Weight: Bodyweight - Lean Body Mass
Body Fat Percentage (Body Fat Weight x 100) / total bodyweight
